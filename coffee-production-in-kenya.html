Vava Coffee is a Kenyan social enterprise reshaping the coffee industry to put power and profits in the hands of smallholder farmers.

By connecting growers directly to premium markets, Vava Coffee helps farmers earn fair wages for their specialty-grade, ethically sourced beans—often more than 16x their previous income. The company pairs bold storytelling and beautiful packaging with a deep commitment to social impact, ensuring farmers are supported from training all the way to market access.
