Wedu is a social enterprise that supports women’s leadership in South and Southeast Asia through mentorship, community, and access to higher education.

Wedu identifies young women with exceptional leadership potential and provides them with lifelong mentorship, a global network of peers and professionals, and affordable tuition loans. Its innovative financing model allows students to pay back what they can only once employed, creating a sustainable cycle of opportunity. By investing in the next generation of changemakers, Wedu is helping build a world where half of all leaders are women.
